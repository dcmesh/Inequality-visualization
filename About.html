New Gilded Age: A Visualization of Wealth Inequality

New Gilded Age is a website designed to provide users with detailed graphs and charts modelling wealth inequality across the world. We used a combination of two key elements for bringing these models to life. The first was a massive amount of public quantitative and qualitative data from highly accredited sources (The United Nations and World Inequality Database). This data consists of information telling the story of the changing financial and economic status of a country/state’s people over approximately the last century. Bringing this data to life in the form of the charts and graphs above was done using Omnisci’s sophisticated cloud database and mapping software, which allowed for us to elegantly draw from hundreds of thousands of tuples of data within lighting fast speeds, and mold them into customized graphics. Using the data sources in conjunction with Omnisci’s software, we have put together what we believe to be a true statistical representation of how humanity has brought upon itself the second Gilded Age.
